# 🖱️ How to Make a New Alias (Super Simple!)

Since I just fixed everything, you should make a **fresh alias** from the updated file.

---

## ✅ Step-by-Step:

### Step 1: Find the File
- Go to your project folder: `Sage Reports`
- Find the file: **"Launch Sage Reports.command"**
- (It has a terminal/script icon)

### Step 2: Make the Alias
1. **Right-click** on "Launch Sage Reports.command"
2. Click **"Make Alias"** (or press `Cmd + L`)
3. A new file will appear called **"Launch Sage Reports.command alias"**

### Step 3: Move It to Desktop
- **Drag** the alias file to your **Desktop**
- Now you can double-click it from your Desktop!

### Step 4: (Optional) Rename It
- Right-click the alias on Desktop
- Click **"Rename"**
- Name it: **"Sage Reports"** (or whatever you want)
- Press Enter

---

## 🎯 Even Better: Add to Dock!

1. **Drag** the alias (or the original file) to your **Dock**
   - The Dock is the bar at the bottom of your screen
2. **Drop it** on the Dock
3. Now you can **click it from your Dock** anytime!

---

## ✅ That's It!

Now you have:
- ✅ A working launcher (I just fixed the dependencies)
- ✅ An alias on your Desktop (easy to find)
- ✅ (Optional) An icon in your Dock (super convenient)

**Just double-click the alias** and the app will start!

**Delete the old alias** if you made one before:
